Favourite Place

TajMahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India.

Golden Temple

The Golden temple is located in the holy city of the Sikhs, Amritsar. The Golden temple is famous for its full golden dome

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a historical palace and a royal residence (house)
